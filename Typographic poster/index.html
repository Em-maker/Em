Pitching Vol. 2

10 . 17 / 7 - 9 PM

Overview


You've done the research, you've put in the face time, how do you close and secure the work? This is a continuation of Pitching, from development to delivery, which explored taking an idea and making it into a winning pitch. From commercial work to personal projects, breaking everything down into bite sized pieces, pricing, personal worth and mental grounding while closing the deal.

Who is it for


You will learn how to approach the conversation, honing in on the intention and ultimately managing the end result: getting paid.

You will learn


This workshop is for you if you’ve started fleshing out an idea and want to learn how to turn it into short, or long term work.
  • How to approach and re-engage your intended client
  • Synthesize your idea into a manageable project
  • What do things actually cost (transparency in invoicing your client)
  • How to under promise and over delivery

    About the facilitator


    Neil Watson is a photographer based in Miami and Toronto. He is dedicated to inclusion and relationship building and uses his connections to push for the widening of perspectives. While carving out his own space within his medium, he has consistently worked to build connections with individuals willing to push boundaries and increase scope in their respective fields. This includes visual artists, advocates for social justice, writers, lawyers, and designers.


    His client list includes National Geographic, Bentley Motors, Knot Standard, Ikiré Jones x Marvel's Black Panther, Vogue Italia, Ebony Magazine, The Rake Magazine, among many more.
